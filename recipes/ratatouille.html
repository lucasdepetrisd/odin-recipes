Ratatouille

Description

Not everyone can become a great artist, but a great artist can come from anywhere.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Red pepper
  • Garlic
  • Pepper
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Onions
  • Tomato
  • Zucchini
  • Salt
  • Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a 10-inch skillet over medium-high heat. Add thinly sliced onions, thinly sliced zucchini, chopped red peppers, and salt. Cook for 8 minutes, stirring occasionally until the vegetables are slightly tender.
  2. Stir in chopped tomatoes, minced garlic, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper and cook, stirring occasionally, 3 minutes.
  3. Transfer the ratatouille to a bowl or a plate and top with grated or shredded Parmesan cheese, if desired.
